About Us

Your Trusted Pet Specialists Since 2010

Aaditya's Pets Villa is a family-owned business dedicated to providing healthy, happy pets and premium pet supplies. We specialize in breeding exceptional quality dogs with outstanding temperaments while offering the best organic pet food and accessories.

All our breeding dogs are genetically tested and certified, and we provide complete veterinary records for every puppy. We follow strict ethical breeding practices and are committed to the lifelong wellbeing of all our animals.
